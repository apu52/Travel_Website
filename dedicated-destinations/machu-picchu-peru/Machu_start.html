Things To Do

Machu Picchu has temples, residences, platforms, and roads, and most of these are included in the tourist route with a guide.

Some places, like the Intipunku and Inca Bridge, require optional routes to enter, while others, like the Temple of the Moon, require a different ticket. The most popular way to get to Machu Picchu is by hiking the 4-day Inca Trail, which takes hikers through the Andes, past lesser-known Inca sites, and ends at the Sun Gate. There's also a 2-day Inca Trail for those who want a shorter or less challenging hike.

The trail includes steep stone staircases and narrow paths, and hikers feel a sense of accomplishment when they reach the Sun Gate.
