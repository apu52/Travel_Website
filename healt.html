Health & Safety Alerts

Please review the health and safety alerts before traveling.

Disease Outbreaks
Infectious diseases like COVID-19 or malaria may be present. Check vaccinations.
Vaccination Requirements
Some destinations require vaccines for yellow fever, typhoid, etc.
Food & Water Safety
Avoid street food, drink bottled water, and be cautious of food hygiene.
Air Quality Alerts
Poor air quality can affect breathing. Wear masks if needed.
Extreme Weather
Heatwaves, cold snaps, and high humidity can be dangerous. Stay hydrated.
Road Safety Warnings
Drive safely. Watch for accident-prone zones and bad roads.
Natural Disasters
Check for risks like earthquakes, floods, hurricanes before travel.
Crime & Security Risks
Avoid high-crime areas, beware of pickpockets and scams.
Transport Disruptions
Flights, buses, or trains may be delayed due to strikes or technical issues.
